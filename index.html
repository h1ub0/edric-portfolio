Courses Availble

Private Driving Lesson

One-on-one in-car sessions tailored to your skill level. Whether you’re a beginner, preparing for the G2 or G road test, or looking to build confidence behind the wheel, I offer patient, clear instruction to help you drive safely and confidently.

Road Test Package

An MTO-approved course that includes 20 hours of online theory, 10 hours of homework, and 10 hours of in-car training. Completing the course may reduce the G2 wait time and could qualify you for an insurance discount. Ideal for first-time drivers.
